🆚 Heated Gloves vs Compression Gloves

Heated gloves are best for:

  • 🌡 Cold-weather arthritis pain
  • 🛌 Bedtime relief from overnight stiffness
  • 💪 Boosting blood flow to inflamed joints

Compression gloves, on the other hand, are better for:

  • 📆 All-day wear
  • 💻 Typing and working with hands
  • 🎯 Preventing light swelling during activities
